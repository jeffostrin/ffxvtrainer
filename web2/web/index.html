The schedule above forecasts which events are coming up based on recent data. A line that looks
  07:00pm (01-15) (in 2:10:14) [ Guild Quests ] [ 25x Luna's Gift Fragment ]
Means that at 7:00 PM (in 2 hours, 10 minutes, and 14 seconds)
  • a new Guild Quest event will start
  • luna will gift 25x Luna's Gift Fragments
NOTE: About 10% of the time the mini-event from the prior hour will happen again. So while Guild Quests may be on the schedule you may also get the prior hour's event.
